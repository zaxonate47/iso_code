Events

The Indian students Organization has a full calendar of cultural, social and professional events through the school year. Our events are open to all and are almost always free of charge

Freshers Welcome: To welcome the scores of Indian students who cross the Atlantic to MSU every year, the ISO kicks its yearly calendar of events off with a general body meeting followed by a party.

Diwali: To celebrate the Indian festival of lights, the ISO brings together the Greater Lansing community for an evening of laughter and exotic Indian cuisine.

Global Festival: The ISO stall at the Global Festival (an annual event to foster international conciousness organized by the Michigan State University) features Mehndi tatoos, Indian handicrafts and galleries of colorful Indian rangolis

Sargam: Our annual play, Sargam has grown from being a small event taking place within the confines of he MSU campus to a musical/histrionic extravaganza that the MSU community looks forward to year after year. Every year, Sargam is featured prominently in MSU's campus newspaper, The State News: Sargam 2011 , Sargam 2010
